The SLOT Project

The Supervised Learning Opportunities by Text project.

Informing and connecting medical students to learning opportunies in realtime within a hospital.

The SLOT project started at an NHS hackday to solve a particular problem where junior doctors often have the opportunity to supervise a medical student through a medical procedure. However, identifying ones in close proximity is a problem. SLOT informs medical students about these opportunities instantly using their mobile phone and lets them connect with those opportunities in real time.
